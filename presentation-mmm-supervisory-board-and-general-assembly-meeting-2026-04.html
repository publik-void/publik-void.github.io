Conclusion

  • DMOs may outperform clinical scores in early-stage ataxia
  • Context-aware analysis improves reliability and sensitivity of real-life DMOs
    • Next up: Reintroduce composite severity scores for further improvement
  • Proper longitudinal modeling yields realistic ESSs for RCTs
  • Single-arm N-of-few trials likely require long durations and frequent measurements
